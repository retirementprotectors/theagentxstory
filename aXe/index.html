One source of truth

Every conversation, every document, every dollar — ingested, transcribed, attributed, analyzed, and locked into one verifiable record, across the entire arc of the business. Most firms capture a slice. We capture the whole arc, end to end, as one source of truth.

Marketing → Prospecting → Opportunity → Close → New business → Commission → Service for life

The marketing that creates the lead → the prospecting that works it → the opportunity that opens → the case that closes → the new business that books → the commission that pays → the service that keeps the client for life. Nothing falls through the cracks, because nothing ever leaves the record.

Under the hood — what your tech team can verify: Every record — from the first marketing touch to the final commission entry — is universally keyed to one client identity, content-addressed and SHA-256-verified on ingestion, and written to an immutable, append-only audit trail with point-in-time recoverability. One client, one cryptographically-fingerprinted record, traceable across the entire financial-services lifecycle.

“Is it blockchain?” — No, and deliberately. A chain adds latency and overhead we don’t need. We get the same verifiable single-record integrity through universal keying, SHA-256 content-addressing, and an append-only audit log — the guarantees without the ledger tax.
